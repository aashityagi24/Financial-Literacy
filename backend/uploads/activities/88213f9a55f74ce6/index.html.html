Money In the Messy Room

You have a very messy sibling and they left money lying around in your room. You have to find and collect all the notes and coins.

  • Click the Start The Hunt on the next page.
  • You will hear the room say “I spy a Rs.10 coin” (or a lion, etc.).
  • Listen carefully and click on the correct animal in the forest scene.
  • If you’re right, the coin/note disappears and you move onto finding the next one.
  • If you’re wrong, try again till you get it right.
