Is This A Need or A Want?

Activity 1: Sorting Needs and Wants

Needs vs Wants

Drag each item card into the correct box to classify them as "Need" or "Want".

Needs = basic things you must have Wants = things that are fun to have

How To Play

  • Read the information on the card.
  • Ask yourself: “Do I need it to stay safe, healthy, or to learn and grow?”
  • Drag it to NEEDS or WANTS based on what you think.
  • Click Check to see your result.
Drag cards. Needs keep us healthy and safe. Wants add fun in our lives.
